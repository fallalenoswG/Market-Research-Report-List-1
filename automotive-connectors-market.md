<p><p>The automotive connectors market is primarily categorized into three types: wire-to-wire connectors, which link two or more wires directly, facilitating flexible interconnections; wire-to-board connectors, which connect wires to printed circuit boards, essential for integrating components; and board-to-board connectors, allowing connections between multiple circuit boards within vehicles, enhancing modularity and system robustness. Each type plays a critical role in ensuring reliable communication and power distribution among automotive electronic systems, supporting advancements in vehicle technology and safety features.</p></p>
