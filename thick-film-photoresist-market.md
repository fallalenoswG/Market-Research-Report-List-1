<p><strong>The Thick Film Photoresist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresists</li><li>Negative Photoresists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thick Film Photoresist market is divided into two main types: positive and negative photoresists. Positive photoresists become soluble upon exposure to light, allowing the desired patterns to be developed, making them ideal for intricate designs. In contrast, negative photoresists harden when exposed to light, leaving behind the unexposed areas after development, which is suitable for applications requiring robust features. Both types are essential in electronics for circuit board fabrication, sensors, and other microelectronic devices, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
