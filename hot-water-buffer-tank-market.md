<p><strong>The Hot Water Buffer Tank Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Buffer Tank</li><li>Air Bag Buffer Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hot Water Buffer Tank market features two primary types: Diaphragm Buffer Tanks and Air Bag Buffer Tanks. Diaphragm Buffer Tanks utilize a flexible membrane to separate water from an inert gas, maintaining system pressure and ensuring efficient hot water delivery. In contrast, Air Bag Buffer Tanks rely on an inflatable bag to cushion fluctuations in water pressure, providing similar benefits in thermal storage and flow regulation. Both types enhance energy efficiency and system longevity in heating applications.</p></p>
