<p><strong>The Beverage Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pump</li><li>Positive Displacement Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beverage pumps market comprises two main types: centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational force to move liquids, making them suitable for low-viscosity beverages and high flow rates. In contrast, positive displacement pumps operate by trapping a fixed amount of liquid and forcing it through the discharge, ideal for handling thicker fluids and achieving precise flow rates. Both types cater to diverse beverage applications, including soft drinks, juices, and alcoholic beverages, ensuring efficient and reliable processing.</p></p>
