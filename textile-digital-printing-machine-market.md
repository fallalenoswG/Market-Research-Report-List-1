<p><p>The textile digital printing machine market encompasses various printing techniques, including sublimation inkjet printing, direct to fabric printing, and direct to garment printing. Sublimation inkjet printing transfers dye onto synthetic fabrics for vibrant, long-lasting designs. Direct to fabric printing directly applies ink onto textiles, suitable for a wide range of materials. Meanwhile, direct to garment printing targets textiles with intricate designs, primarily for apparel, allowing for high-resolution prints and flexibility in design options. Each method serves distinct market needs.</p></p>
