<p><p>Flooded lead-acid batteries for vehicles fall into two primary market types: General Flooded Lead-Acid Batteries and Enhanced Flooded Batteries (EFB). General flooded batteries are traditional, affordable, and widely used in various applications but require regular maintenance. EFBs, on the other hand, feature improved design and better resistance to deeper discharge cycles, making them suitable for start-stop vehicles and enhancing energy efficiency. Both types serve crucial roles in the automotive sector, balancing cost, performance, and maintenance needs.</p></p>
