<p><strong>The RF Coaxial Cable Assemblies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-Rigid</li><li>Semi-Flexible</li><li>Flexible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF coaxial cable assemblies are categorized into three main types based on their flexibility and construction: </p><p>1. **Semi-Rigid** cables offer high performance and minimal signal loss due to their solid outer conductor, but require bending tools for installation. </p><p>2. **Semi-Flexible** cables provide more installation flexibility while maintaining good electrical performance, suitable for applications needing moderate bending. </p><p>3. **Flexible** cables are the most adaptable, ideal for environments with frequent movement, yet may have slightly higher attenuation compared to the other two types.</p></p>
