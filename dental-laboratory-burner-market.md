<p><ul><li>Liquid Gas</li><li>Natural Gas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental laboratory burner market is categorized primarily into two types: liquid gas and natural gas burners. Liquid gas burners utilize propane or butane as fuel, offering portability and high heating efficiency, making them suitable for various dental applications. In contrast, natural gas burners are connected to a natural gas line, providing a consistent and cost-effective heat source for long-term use. Both types are integral for processes like melting and shaping dental alloys, essential in prosthetic and restorative dentistry.</p></p>
