<p><strong>The In Vitro Organ Preservation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Cold Storage</li><li>Hypothermic Machine Perfusion</li><li>Normothermic Machine Perfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The in vitro organ preservation market comprises three key types: </p><p>1. **Static Cold Storage** involves cooling organs at low temperatures to prolong viability without perfusion, but has limitations in duration.</p><p>2. **Hypothermic Machine Perfusion** enhances preservation by actively perfusing organs with cold preservation solutions, improving nutrient supply and removing waste.</p><p>3. **Normothermic Machine Perfusion** maintains organs at body temperature, allowing for metabolism and assessing function, leading to potentially better outcomes for transplantation.</p></p>
