<p><p>The online diagram software market is primarily categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software operates on remote servers, allowing users to access their diagrams from anywhere with an internet connection. This model typically features collaborative tools and automatic updates. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed directly on local machines, offering greater control over data security and customization. Each type caters to different organizational needs and preferences regarding accessibility and data management.</p></p>
