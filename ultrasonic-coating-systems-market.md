<p><p>Ultrasonic coating systems are advanced technologies used for precise application of coatings in various industries. Rotary-type ultrasonic coating systems utilize a rotating substrate to achieve uniform coating application, ideal for cylindrical or irregular shapes. In contrast, flat-type ultrasonic coating systems are designed for flat surfaces, ensuring even distribution of coatings on sheets or panels. Both types leverage ultrasonic vibrations to enhance coating adhesion, reduce material waste, and improve process efficiency, catering to diverse manufacturing needs.</p></p>
