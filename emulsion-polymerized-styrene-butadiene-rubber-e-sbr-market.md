<p><p>Emulsion Polymerized Styrene-butadiene Rubber (E-SBR) is a synthetic rubber known for its excellent wear resistance and flexibility. The market can be segmented into two main types: Oil-Extended E-SBR and Non-Oil Extended E-SBR. Oil-Extended E-SBR incorporates processing oils to enhance compatibility and reduce costs, improving its performance in applications like tires. In contrast, Non-Oil Extended E-SBR lacks these oils, resulting in superior heat resistance and tensile strength, making it suitable for high-performance applications in industries such as automotive and footwear.</p></p>
