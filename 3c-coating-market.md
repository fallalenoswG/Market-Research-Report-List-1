<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Coating</li><li>UV Curing Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3C coating market encompasses various types, including thermosetting coatings and UV curing coatings. Thermosetting coatings undergo a chemical reaction when heated, resulting in a durable, heat-resistant finish suitable for automotive and industrial applications. In contrast, UV curing coatings utilize ultraviolet light to rapidly cure, offering quick drying times and enhanced durability, commonly used in electronics and wood finishing. Both types provide high-performance solutions tailored to specific market needs, contributing to advancements in surface protection and aesthetics.</p></p>
