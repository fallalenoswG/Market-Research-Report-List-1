<p><p>The Molded Goods Market encompasses various types, including Liquid Injection Molding (LIM) and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). LIM involves injecting liquid thermoplastic materials into molds, enabling complex shapes and high precision, ideal for components in automotive and electronics industries. LSR, on the other hand, focuses on high-performance silicone materials, providing flexibility, durability, and resistance to extreme temperatures. Both processes cater to diverse applications, offering efficient production methods and enhancing product versatility in various sectors, including healthcare and consumer goods.</p></p>
