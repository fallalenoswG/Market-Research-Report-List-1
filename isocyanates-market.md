<p><p>The isocyanates market includes three primary types: Methyl Diphenyl Diisocyanate (MDI), Toluene Diisocyanate (TDI), and Aliphatic Diisocyanate (ADI). MDI is mainly used in rigid foams and insulation, while TDI is commonly found in flexible foams and automotive applications. Aliphatic diisocyanates are typically utilized in coatings and adhesives due to their stability and weather resistance. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, shaping their respective market dynamics and influencing production and demand across various sectors, particularly construction and automotive industries.</p></p>
