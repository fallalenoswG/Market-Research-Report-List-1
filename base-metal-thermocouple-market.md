<p><p>The base metal thermocouple market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Type J thermocouples, composed of iron and constantan, are ideal for moderate temperature ranges. Type K, made from chromel and alumel, is widely used due to its versatility and broad temperature range. Type N thermocouples offer improved stability and oxidation resistance at higher temperatures. Type E, featuring chromel and constantan, is known for its high output and precision. Type T, made from copper and constantan, excels in lower temperature applications. Each type has distinct properties that cater to various industrial needs.</p></p>
