<p><p>The engine filter market comprises three primary types: air filters, oil filters, and fuel filters. Air filters prevent dust and contaminants from entering the engine, ensuring optimal air intake. Oil filters remove impurities from engine oil, maintaining lubrication and performance. Fuel filters clean the fuel before it reaches the engine, preventing particles from causing damage that could affect efficiency. Together, these filters play crucial roles in enhancing engine performance, longevity, and overall vehicle reliability, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
