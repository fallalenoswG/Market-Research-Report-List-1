<p><strong>The Portable Automobile Exhaust Gas Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable automobile exhaust gas analyzer market comprises two main types: capacitive and semiconductor analyzers. Capacitive analyzers measure gas concentrations by detecting changes in capacitance caused by gas absorption, offering high sensitivity and stability. In contrast, semiconductor analyzers utilize metal oxide sensors that change resistance in the presence of specific gases, enabling low-cost and compact solutions. Both types provide essential data for monitoring vehicle emissions, ensuring regulatory compliance, and enhancing environmental protection in the automotive industry.</p></p>
