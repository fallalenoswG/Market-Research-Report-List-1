<p><p>The servo gearmotors market comprises various types, notably parallel shaft and bevel gearmotors. Parallel shaft gearmotors feature shafts aligned in parallel, providing compact designs ideal for high-torque applications and efficient power transmission. Bevel gearmotors, on the other hand, utilize angled gears to change the axis of rotation, enabling versatile mounting options and space-saving configurations. Both types are integral in automation, robotics, and precision machinery, catering to diverse industries seeking reliable motion control solutions.</p></p>
