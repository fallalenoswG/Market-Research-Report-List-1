<p><p>The Low PIM Connector market includes three main types: Wire to Wire, Wire to Board, and Board to Board. Wire to Wire connectors enable direct connections between two cables, ideal for signal integrity in high-frequency applications. Wire to Board connectors link wires to circuit boards, ensuring reliable signal transmission while saving space. Board to Board connectors facilitate connections between multiple circuit boards, enhancing design flexibility and system integration. Each type focuses on minimizing passive intermodulation (PIM) for superior performance in telecommunications.</p></p>
