<p><ul><li>Pump-Line-Nozzle Delivery Systems</li><li>Common Rail Delivery Systems</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive fuel delivery systems market consists of several types. Pump-line-nozzle delivery systems utilize a mechanical pump to transfer fuel to the engine through a series of lines and nozzles, ensuring precise fuel atomization. Common rail delivery systems, often found in diesel engines, feature a shared rail where fuel is stored at high pressure, allowing for multiple injectors to draw fuel as needed for optimal combustion. Other market types may include direct injection and port fuel injection systems, each offering unique performance benefits.</p></p>
