<p><strong>The Folding Screen Mobile Phone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Fold</li><li>Out Fold</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The folding screen mobile phone market is primarily categorized into two types: inner fold and out fold. Inner fold devices feature a display that folds inward, protecting the screen when closed and offering a compact design. In contrast, out fold models have the display that folds outward, allowing for a larger visible screen but potentially exposing it to damage. Both designs aim to enhance portability and user experience, catering to different preferences and usage scenarios in the smartphone market.</p></p>
